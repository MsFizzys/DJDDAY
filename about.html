About

Dylan Day

Based in Petaluma, DJDDAY brings House and Electronic sound to the North Bay — a region where that scene has been mostly overlooked in favor of San Francisco's bigger stages. Dylan started building sets that blend deep, driving rhythms with a feel for the room, developing a sound shaped as much by Sonoma and Marin's laid-back energy as by the genre's underground roots.

Whether it's a packed club floor or an outdoor gathering under string lights, DJDDAY brings the same intention: read the crowd, build the energy, keep it moving. With a growing catalog of mixes and a home base right in the North Bay, DJDDAY is bringing House music to a region that's ready for it.

— Dylan
